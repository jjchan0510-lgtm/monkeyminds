How to Play

Objective: Defeat monsters by spelling words correctly!

  1. Listen to the word pronunciation
  2. Read the word definition
  3. Type the correct spelling
  4. Press Enter or click "Check" to submit
  5. Correct spelling = Monster takes damage
  6. Defeat all monsters to advance to the next level

Tips:

  • Use the "Hint" button for clues
  • Use "Pronounce" to hear the word again
  • Combo correct answers for bonus damage!
